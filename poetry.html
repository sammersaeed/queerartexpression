Day by Day by Sammer Saeed

Everyday is the same,
But today is a new day,
It needs to be me that I portray,
I will not be the one to blame,

Everyday is the same,
But today is a new day,
It can’t be that part of me hidden away,
I will not be the one to tame,

Everyday I am not myself,
It needs to be different,
Just to shout that I am not a she,

Everyday I tell myself,
My lack of gender is magnificent,
So today is the day, I choose to be me
